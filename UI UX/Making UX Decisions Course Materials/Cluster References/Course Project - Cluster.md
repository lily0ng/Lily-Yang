## Background

You are a product designer at Cluster, a small but ambitious startup that has just secured its seed funding of $2 million. The company, founded by two ex-Google employees, aims to revolutionize the content production industry with its innovative tool, Cluster.

## Your Role

As the sole product designer on a lean team of 8 (2 founders, 3 developers, 1 product manager, 1 marketer, and you), you're responsible for shaping the entire user experience of Cluster. You report directly to the CEO, who has a strong product vision but limited design experience.

## Company Situation

Cluster is operating in a highly competitive market with established players like Canva, Notion, and Airtable. The founders believe that Cluster's unique AI-powered summarization feature and its focus on content production workflows will give it a significant edge. However, the company is under pressure to launch an MVP within the next 3 months to start generating early revenue and prepare for the next funding round.
